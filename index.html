About · references · privacy

DeepRobin

A tool that estimates the depth of a photo and simulates the blur of a fast lens. Depth estimation runs entirely in your browser. The blur is computed in WebGL as a disc sampling in linear light, with background highlights blooming into bokeh.

Privacy

  • No account, no personal data. The app never asks who you are.
  • No uploads. Your photos are processed in your browser and are never sent to, or stored on, any server.
  • Nothing is kept. Images live only in this tab and disappear when you close it.
  • Only one network request: a one-time download of the open depth model, which is then cached on your device.

Depth model

  • Depth Anything V2 — the model that makes single-image depth possible. Thanks to its authors for open-sourcing the work: github.com/DepthAnything/Depth-Anything-V2
  • Browser-ready ONNX build, packaged by the community: onnx-community/depth-anything-v2-small

In-browser runtime

  • Transformers.js (Hugging Face) — runs the model with WebGPU or WASM, no server: github.com/huggingface/transformers.js
